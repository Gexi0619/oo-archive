Christy 28 Oct 2002, 13:43

Dez/Etienne

I agree that you see strong plus lenses on little kids and old people. In the case of old people - it's usually because the muscles controlling their eye lenses have siezed up. The condition is known as presbyopia and it generally becomes apparent some time after the mid-40s. It leaves people unable to focus on close-up things without the use of plus lenses. In the case of little kids - I believe eye tests are picking up hyperopia more often than in previous years. Hyperopic kids can usually function fairly well without glasses - up to a certain point - but wearing glasses certainly relieves them of some of the strain of accommodating all the time. As for astigmatism - I have -2.00 of astigmatism and I don't think anyone could notice that while I'm wearing my glasses. However - anyone looking through my glasses while rotating the lenses would notice the image distorting significantly while they did it.
