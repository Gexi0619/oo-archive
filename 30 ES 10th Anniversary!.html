Today (March 13th), is the 5th anniversary of Eye Scene! ES launched as an e-zine on 13 March 1997 and the first message board, which launched in April of that year, gradually took over until ES became the discussion and chat site it now is.

So let us now indulge in a few reminiscences...

Personally I have had tons of great times on ES. Hanging out with the regulars on LensChat until the wee hours, debating various and sundry issues on the BBS.

I've traded pictures, links, email addresses, vintage eyewear, stories, webmastering tips and goodness knows what else.

In addition to lynnie (of course) I've had the pleasure of meeting several of you in real life and that is another favorite side of ES for me. I also enjoy hearing about other ES users getting together face-to-face, as many have over the years.

Anyway, here is a thread to celebrate the past five years and to wax a bit nostalgic. For those who have been with the site since 13 March 1997 and the many souls who have wandered in since: cheers to you!!!

a few statistics...

- versions of message system: 3

- approximate hits to ES front page: 750,000

- approximate message posts: 18,000+

- very approximate chat posts: 600,000

- most fecund link to ES: http://dir.yahoo.com/Society_and_Culture/Sexuality/Fetishes/Glasses/
